HP Laptop Store – Website Project Plan

Site Name

HP Laptop Store

This name clearly communicates that the website specializes in showcasing and selling HP laptop models. It is simple, recognizable, and directly tied to the products the site offers.

Optional Domain: hplaptopstore.com (example)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to display a wide range of HP laptops in an organized, e-commerce-style layout. Users can explore laptop models, filter by processor type, read detailed product information, and learn about warranties and purchasing tips. A contact form will allow customers to make inquiries.

Scenarios

  • Which HP laptops are available with Intel Core i5 processors?
  • How much do HP laptops cost, and what are the specifications for each model?
  • Where can I contact the store for warranty and support information?

Color Scheme

Below are the selected colors and where they will be used on the website:

Primary Color – Deep HP Blue (#004aad)

Used for: headings, navigation bar, buttons, highlights

Secondary Color – Metallic Silver (#b7c4c8)

Used for: card backgrounds, borders, subtle accents

Typography

Font Family: Poppins

  • Poppins Bold (700): Page titles and headings
  • Poppins Medium (500): Navigation items and section labels
  • Poppins Light (300): Body text and product descriptions
